Pareto

OPERATIONAL

iOS bill-splitting app — scan a receipt with AI, assign items to each person, auto-split tax & tip, and request payment via Venmo.

▸ Download on the App Store
EXHIBIT — PARETO
▌ Overview

Split the bill effortlessly. Pareto turns the awkward post-dinner math into a few taps: snap a photo of the receipt, let AI read the line items, assign each dish to whoever ordered it, and Pareto works out exactly what everyone owes — tax and tip included — then fires off payment requests over Venmo.

No more "just split it evenly" when three people only had water. Add your guests, drag items onto their cards, and watch each person's total and share-of-the-bill update live. When the numbers are settled, collect with a single tap.

▌ Field Capabilities
  • Tap-to-assign. Assign menu items to each guest with a single tap — no manual math, no spreadsheets.
  • Live per-person totals. Automatic per-person totals and share percentages recalculate the instant anything changes.
  • Scan & split. Add your guests, snap the receipt, and split the whole thing instantly with AI line-item detection.
▌ Known Capabilities
  • SwiftUI
  • Gemini AI (receipt OCR)
  • Venmo deep links
  • StoreKit (Pro)
  • iMessage extension
